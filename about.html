Our Story

PASSION. PURPOSE.
PERFECTION.

Infamous Team Reed Bullies is a family-run, Christian-based kennel in Owasso, Oklahoma, founded by Terrance Reed Sr. What began as a passion for the American Bully has grown into one of the Midwest's most respected programs, built on champion foundation stock and puppies placed in loving homes across the country.

Today that program is carried by our current generation — studs Duke, Luke Cage, Matrix, and Ryder, and our foundation females Diva, Isabella, and Puma — and it keeps proving itself in the ring, most recently bringing home Best of Breed and Best In Show honors at the ABKC Oklahoma Bully Breed Expo 6 in Shawnee (2025). Every breeding is planned for elite bloodlines, unmatched structure, and superior temperament.
